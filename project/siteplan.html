Site Purpose

The site is for Nara Dental clinic. A general dental practice located in Lagos, South western Nigeria. The site provides detailed information on booking, pricing, locations and our team of dedicated staffs.

Scenarios

What services do you offer at Nara dental specialties?

At Nara Dental clinic we offer a wide range of oral health services ranging from general dental consultations, prophylaxis, cavity fillings, removable and fixed dental prosthesis, oral surgical procedures and paediatric dental care

How do i book an appointment?

You can visit our booking page to fill in your details and book an appointment instantly or you can call our customer care contact for a more personalized appointment booking.

Can i get my wisdom tooth extracted at your clinic without feeling any pain?

At our clinic, we try our best to provide painless and comfortable oral surgical services. We provide you with adequate anesthesia to ensure smooth disimpactio of wisdom tooth without inflicting unnecessary discomfort. Visit us today for a proper consultation.
